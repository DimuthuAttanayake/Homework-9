# Homework Assignments

These are homework assignments for [Computing Foundations](https://journalism.columbia.edu/ms-data-journalism), Fall 2025.

## Assignment 09

This is the introductory assignment on data scraping with Python.

* [Assignment 1](scraping_apples.ipynb) - Scraping the apple varieties from the [New York Apple Association](https://www.applesfromny.com/varieties/)  
* [Assignment 2](scraping-prison_%20facilities.ipynb) - Scraping the prison facility locations from the [Louisiana Department of Corrections](https://doc.la.gov/about-the-dpsc/facility-locations/)  
* [Assignment 3](scraping_gov_funding.ipynb) - Scraping Arkansas State Police Commission meeting minutes from the [Arkansas State Police Commission](https://dps.arkansas.gov/law-enforcement/arkansas-state-police/directors-office/asp-commission/asp-commission-meeting-minutes/)  
* [Assignment 4](scraping_meeting_minutes.ipynb) - Scraping the first 10 pages of National Institute of Justice funding awards from the [NIJ Awards Portal](https://nij.ojp.gov/funding/awards/list)
